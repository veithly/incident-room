# Incident Room Terms of Use

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Incident Room is a Reddit Developer Platform app for subreddit moderators. By installing or using the app, you agree to use it only for moderation workflows in communities where you have the required moderator permissions.

## Acceptable use

- Use Incident Room to collect incident evidence, coordinate review, and keep an audit timeline.
- Review all evidence before taking Reddit-side moderation action.
- Follow Reddit's rules, the Reddit Developer Terms, and your community rules.
- Do not use the app to harass, profile, or target users outside legitimate moderation work.
- Do not treat AI briefing text as a final moderation decision.

## AI output

Incident Room may call an OpenAI-compatible model to summarize selected incident evidence. The model output is only a briefing. It can be incomplete or wrong. Moderators remain responsible for every moderation decision.

## No automatic enforcement

Incident Room previews action packs and records confirmation state. It does not grant the AI model authority to remove, lock, ban, approve, or otherwise mutate Reddit content.

## Availability

This hackathon build is provided as-is. It may change, fail, or be removed without notice.
